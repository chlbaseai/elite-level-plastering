About Us

Built on precision,
proven by results

Elite Level Plastering delivers professional internal and external plastering services across Surrey and South London. Every wall we finish is a reflection of our standards — smooth, clean, and built to last.

We use adhesive-fixed corner beads rather than staples, giving a stronger bond and a cleaner finish that won’t crack or shift over time. It costs more in materials and time, but the result speaks for itself.

Our Process

Three stages to a
flawless finish

Every job follows the same proven process. Preparation is the foundation of quality — we never cut corners to save time.

01

Preparation

Corner beads applied with drywall adhesive — never stapled. This method prevents uneven finishes and ensures the edge stays solid for years. Preparation is the key to a quality finish.

02

Undercoat

Fills holes and levels uneven surfaces using bonding, hardwall, plasterboard, or sand and cement as needed. This creates the solid foundation for a smooth top coat.

03

Top Coat & Skim

Two-coat application as standard — delivering a much cleaner finish. The result is a smooth surface ready for any level of painting and decorating work.
