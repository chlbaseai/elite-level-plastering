# Internal Plastering Services

## Overview
Elite Level Plastering offers professional internal plastering services with a three-stage process.

## The Three Stages

### 1. Preparation
"Preparation is the key to a quality finish."
- Corner beads applied with drywall adhesive (not stapled)
- Adhesive application prevents uneven finishes and ensures superior durability
- Proper preparation ensures the final result is strong and long-lasting

### 2. Undercoat
- Fills holes and levels uneven surfaces
- Materials used as appropriate: bonding, hardwall, plasterboard, sand/cement
- Creates the foundation for a smooth top coat

### 3. Top Coat / Skimming
- Two-coat application (industry standard)
- Provides "a much cleaner finish"
- Results in a smooth surface ready for painting and decorating
- Suitable for any level of painting/decorating work

## Key Differentiators
- Use of drywall adhesive for corner beads rather than stapling — this is highlighted as a major quality differentiator
- Two-coat finishing as standard
- Emphasis on preparation as the foundation of quality
